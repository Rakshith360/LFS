About Little Flower High School

Our Little Flower High School

Founded in 2000, Little Flower High School Academy has a long and distinguished history of providing exceptional education. We blend time-honored traditions with innovative teaching methodologies to foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love of learning.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower students to achieve their full potential and become compassionate, responsible global citizens. We envision a community where every student is inspired to lead, innovate, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Academic Programs

At LFS, we offer a comprehensive and rigorous academic curriculum designed to challenge and inspire students at every level. Our programs emphasize critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration.

Primary School (Ages 5-10)

A nurturing environment fostering foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and inquiry-based learning. Emphasis on holistic development through arts, music, and physical education.

Middle School (Ages 11-14)

A dynamic curriculum that encourages exploration and critical thinking. Students engage in advanced subjects, collaborative projects, and develop leadership skills.

Senior School (Ages 15-18)

Rigorous academic pathways including national and international baccalaureate programs. Focus on university preparation, specialized studies, and independent research.

Royal Arts & Sciences

Specialized programs in visual arts, performing arts, advanced sciences, and humanities, allowing students to pursue their passions with expert guidance.

Coding

Specialized programs in software development, web design, data science, and problem-solving, allowing students to build their tech skills with expert guidance.

sports

Specialized programs in athletics, team sports, and physical fitness, designed to develop discipline, teamwork, and peak physical performance under expert coaching.

Admissions

Joining the LFS Family

We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn, contribute to our vibrant community, and uphold our values of excellence and integrity. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our challenging and supportive environment.

Key Dates & Deadlines:

  • Application Opens: September 1st
  • Application Deadline: January 15th
  • Entrance Examinations: February (various dates)
  • Interviews: March
  • Decision Notification: April 1st

For detailed information on the application process, required documents, and scholarship opportunities, please download our admissions brochure or contact the admissions office.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the age requirements for entry?

For our Primary School, children should be 5 years old by September 1st of the entry year. Specific age requirements for other levels are detailed in our admissions guide.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, LFS offers a range of academic, arts, and sports scholarships based on merit and, in some cases, financial need. Please refer to the scholarships section of our website or brochure.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We pride ourselves on maintaining small class sizes to ensure personalized attention. The average student-teacher ratio is 12:1 across the academy.
